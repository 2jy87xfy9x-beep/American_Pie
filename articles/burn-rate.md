## What It Is

Burn rate is how much money leaves your life every month without pretending income will cover it later. It is rent, food, insurance minimums, the train pass, the prescriptions, and every subscription that renews whether you opened the app or not.

In March 2008, people who could quote their salary but not their burn were already behind. Employers were trimming hours before they trimmed headcount.

## What People Did

**What most people did**

They updated burn only after a crisis — a declined card, a rent check that almost bounced — then rounded to “about two thousand” because shame made precision feel nosy.

**What financially stable people did**

They reconciled burn against bank outflows quarterly, split fixed vs discretionary, and knew which line items could freeze in seven days without negotiation.
