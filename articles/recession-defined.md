## What It Is

A recession is often dated by the National Bureau of Economic Research as a broad, sustained decline in economic activity — not two down quarters of GDP alone, though that rule-of-thumb persists in headlines.

Your wallet can enter recession months before any committee press release confirms it. Hours cut, tips down, commissions vanish — the technical definition lags the felt one.
