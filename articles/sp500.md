## What It Is

The S&P 500 tracks roughly five hundred large U.S. companies weighted mostly by market capitalization — not equal slices — so the biggest names sway the tape disproportionately.

It is a shorthand for “how big U.S. business did this quarter,” not a synonym for your diversified life.
