## What It Is

Many employers match part of your 401(k) deferrals — instant return on payroll you were going to earn anyway, subject to vesting schedules.

## The Math

If employer matches **50%** up to **6%** of salary and you earn **$60,000**, max match **$1,800** free if you defer **$3,600** — **50% instantaneous gain** on that slice before market growth.

Miss years of match? Lost **$1,800 × years** compounded — opportunity cost bigger than skipping lattes.
