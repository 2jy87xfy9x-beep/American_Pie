## What It Is

Fiscal stimulus is Congress and the Treasury pushing money or tax cuts into households hoping demand does not collapse faster than factories can restart.

2009’s packages mixed tax rebates, infrastructure bridges, and safety-net extensions — design mattered as much as headline billions.
