## What It Is

Sequence risk is bad luck timing — taking withdrawals early in a bear market permanently impairs a portfolio versus the same average return with good early years.

Accumulators care less; retirees drawing **{{your_monthly_expenses}}** from **{{your_investment_value}}** care immensely.
