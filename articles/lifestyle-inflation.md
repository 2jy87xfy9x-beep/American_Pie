## What It Is

Lifestyle inflation is when spending rises with income — new apartment, nicer car, imported gym — while net **{{your_net_worth}}** barely moves despite raises.

Raises feel like winning while runway **{{coverage_days}}** stays flat because **{{your_monthly_expenses}}** ate the delta.

## The Math

If income rises **10%** but expenses rise **9%**, savings rate barely improves — if income **10%**, expenses **11%**, you’re backwards broke faster.

Track **Δ{{your_monthly_expenses}}** / **Δ{{your_monthly_income}}** after last raise — ratio **>0.8** signals creep.
