## What It Is

A bear market is traditionally a 20%+ drop from recent highs in a major index — sustained pessimism where buyers disappear and every rally gets sold.

In 2008 the print arrived after families had already been living bear-market emotions for months via layoffs and home equity lines freezing.

**What most people did**

They sold after the damage to stop the feeling, not because the plan changed.
